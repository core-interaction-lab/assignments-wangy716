Manifesto Poster

Poster Design

For this project, students first chose their interested manifesto (over a hundred words). Then, they designed a 700mm x 1000mm poster with a specific event that fit the chosen manifesto. I decided on Martin Luther King Junior's I HAVE A DREAM speech about designing for a gallery exhibition of the civil rights movement at MET. The background design is a meteor flying, demonstrating King's power in his speech.

poster size：50cm x 35xm
soical media: Instagram, website
software use：Indesign, Illustrator, After Effect
