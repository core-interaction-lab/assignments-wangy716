Women Help Women

Poster Design, Digital Animation

For this project, students are asked to create a fictional event for a social campaign (real or imagined) that advocates for a cause that is personally meaningful to them. Then, students create a series of materials promoting this social campaign event. I choose the topic of "Stop Violence Against Women" by utilizing black and white stripes to raise people's awareness, especially women. The poster has two ways of interpretation - "women help" and "help women."

poster size：50cm x 35xm
soical media: Instagram, website
software use：Indesign, Illustrator, After Effect
