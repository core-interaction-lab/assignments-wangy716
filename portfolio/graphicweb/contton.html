Cotton?

Booklet Deisgn

For this project, students are asked to research cotton sustainability problems. I was surprised to find out how much pollution is caused during cotton's growth, including desertification, air pollution, Salinization, water pollution, and soil compacting. Then, I created five collage pieces to build up a booklet for distribution to raise people's awareness.

booklet size：50cm x 10cm
software use：Indesign, Illustrator
