PROJECT: Patient#1843-01
"The Tell-Tale Heart" is a short story written by Edgar Allan Poe, published in 1843. The story is told by an unnamed narrator who endeavors to convince readers of his sanity while describing a murder he committed. In this project, I make the narrator becomes a patient at Saint. Heart Asylum talks about the story so that readers can directly feel the tension and emotion. Also, I applied black squares to hide important information (redacted document) to let readers explore and interact more with the text. - Yi Wang
