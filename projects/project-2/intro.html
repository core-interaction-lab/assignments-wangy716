PROJECT: Patient#1843-01
"The Tell-Tale Heart" is a short story written by Edgar Allan Poe, published in 1843. The story is related by an unnamed narrator who endeavors to convince readers of his/her sanity while describing a murder the narrator committed. Therefore, I focus on the first person point of view of the story to let readers directly face to the main character telling story by designing the font size and spacing between sentences to mimic talking emotion and breath breaks. - Yi Wang
